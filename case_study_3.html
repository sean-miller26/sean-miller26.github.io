Goals

This project aims to transform the Subject page into an interactive hub that supports deeper engagement and insight. Key goals include lowering the barrier to participation by making interactions like tagging and exploration more approachable, and promoting serendipitous discovery by surfacing related subjects and patterns. For advanced users, the goal is to accelerate research workflows by integrating key metadata and tools directly into the interface, reducing friction and enabling more efficient analysis. Ultimately, the page should support both casual curiosity and rigorous scientific inquiry.

Research

I led a brainstorming workshop with researchers from multiple projects to map out overlapping needs and potential solutions. I interviewed advanced volunteers, who often supplement their classifications with external tools like SIMBAD (for object ID and classifications), VizieR (for catalogs and photometry), and WISEView (for infrared imagery). While some users are hesitant to comment, research suggests tagging and metadata exploration would be widely adopted.
